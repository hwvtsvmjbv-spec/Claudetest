What It Is

Remove the defects,
restore the depth.

Over time, washing and the environment leave your paint covered in fine swirls and scratches that scatter light and dull its reflection.

Paint correction uses professional polishing machines and graded compounds to carefully level those imperfections, bringing back the mirror-like finish your car had when it was new.

Why Correct

A finish worth
protecting.

Correction is the difference between paint that looks "okay" and paint that genuinely turns heads — and it’s the essential first step before any coating.
