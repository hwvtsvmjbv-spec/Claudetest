What It Is

A second skin
for your paint.

A ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds to your clear coat, forming a hard, glass-like layer far tougher than any wax.

The result is a richer, glossier finish that repels water and grime, resists etching and oxidation, and makes everyday washing dramatically easier — all backed by a manufacturer warranty.
